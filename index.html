ABOUT US

Lifano Foods introduces an exceptional new concept to bring you the best delicacies and qualities of Anatolia and Mediterranean. We are offering a cornucopia of Mediterranean’s authentic and quality foods under our brand. Sweets and desserts have always been an important and distinctive element of Mediterranean cuisine. That was true in ancient times and is just as true today.
